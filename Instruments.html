Instruments

Guitar

The guitar is a string instrument which is played by pluking by the strings.The main parts of the guitar are the body,the fretboard,the headstock and the strings.

Piano

The piano is an acoustic, stringed musical instrument invented in Italy by Bartolomeo Cristofori around the year 1700, in which the strings are struck by wooden hammers that are coated with a softer material.

Drum

The drum is a member of the percussion group of musical instruments. In the Hornbostel-Sachs classification system, it is a membranophone.
